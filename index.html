STUDENTS ONLINE REGISTRATION DURING THE COVID-19 LOCKDOWN

        Due to the closure of the Institute in compliance with the Government directive as part of measures to contain/prevent the spread of the Coronavirus
        (COVID-19), the Management has directed that students can pay their fees online and complete their registration process from their homes.

       

The payment process flow.

        Student’s logon to the portal (portal.imt.edu.ng) and generate payment Invoice.      

  • pay click here      
  • Put your reference number (Invoice number), select the payment item,and make payment.
  • Return to the portal and print your receipt.

    Students are also advised to register their courses online.
    Click here to make the necessary payments.

  • Return to the portal, verify your pin and print your course registration form.
